The Best Movies According to Steph

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Parasite

A 2019 South Korean dark comedy thriller that explores greed and social class divides.

Get Out

A 2017 horror comedy movie that tackles traditional notions of racism and the darkest aspects of human nature.

Insidious: Chapter 2

Following the events of Chapter 1, this 2013 horror movie continues the story about the evil possessions hauting the Lamberts.
